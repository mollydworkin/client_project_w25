Tutoring at UMSI

UMSI is committed to supporting undergraduate students through a variety of tutoring services.

Whether you need help with coursework or are looking to strengthen your study habits, we are here to assist you every step of the way.

UMSI Peer Tutoring Program

The UMSI Peer Tutoring program, which is run in collaboration with our UMSI faculty, gives UMSI students and students enrolled in UMSI courses the chance to engage with peer tutors and receive help and support in UMSI courses.

Both programming and math tutoring are offered through this service. Use the following links too schedule a tutoring appointment with our available UMSI Peer Tutors:

UMSI Peer-to-Peer Tutor Directory

The Peer-to-Peer Tutor Directory was created to assist students seeking to provide general tutoring. If you are looking for more individualized support, this might be the approach for you. Please note that a fee may be associated with these services, which is determined by the tutor. UMSI just keeps the Peer-to-Peer Tutor Directory up to date every term; it has no control over the tutors listed there.

Drop-In Academic Success Coaching

During the Winter 2025 term, you can attend drop-in academic success coaching sessions to meet one-on-one with our Graduate Academic Success Intern. These sessions are a great chance to get support on topics like:

  • Time management
  • Study strategies
  • Making the most of campus resources
  • Adjusting to university life
  • And any other questions you might have!

Drop-in hours take place on Thursdays from 10-11 AM via Zoom during the Winter 2025 semester.
