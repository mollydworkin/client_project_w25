Academic Support at UMSI

Overview

Academic advising is an essential part of your journey at UMSI. With the guidance of an academic advisor, you can create a personalized academic plan, understand program requirements, and navigate challenges that arise during your studies. Together, we can ensure you are on track to meet both your academic and career goals by:

  • Creating an individualized academic plan
  • Interpreting academic policies or procedures
  • Discussing concerns about a particular course
  • Connecting with student support resources and services
  • Helping you navigate disruptions in your BSI experience

Types of Advising Appointments

We offer different types of advising appointments to cater to your needs:

  • Scheduled 1:1 Appointments: Ideal for in-depth discussions such as course planning, academic progress, or dual degree considerations. These appointments are available in 30-minute increments and must be booked through Navigate360
  • Drop-In 1:1 Appointments: For quick discussions that can be resolved in 10 minutes or less. Drop-ins are available during office hours without the need for a scheduled appointment.
  • Email: If you prefer written communication, you can email the advising team instead.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Advising Appointment

Maximize your advising experience with these tips:

  1. Identify the Appropriate Service: Choose the right type of appointment based on the complexity of your issue.
  2. Be Aware of Deadlines: Schedule your appointments ahead of time to avoid last-minute issues.
  3. Prepare for Your Appointment: Review your degree audit, list your questions, and ensure you're on time for the meeting.
  4. Follow Through: Complete any tasks your advisor suggests after the meeting.
